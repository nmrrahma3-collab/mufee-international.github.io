About Mufee International

Mufee International (PVT) LTD is a Sri Lanka–based cosmetic import and distribution company delivering internationally certified beauty and personal care products with strict regulatory compliance.

Built on professionalism, transparency, and strong global partnerships, we support brands entering and expanding across evolving markets.
